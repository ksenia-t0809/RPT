energy

The Energy sector is undoubtedly one of the most important industries around the world, and one of those leading the industrial growth over the past century by ‘fuelling’ the rest of the economy

The growing importance of the Eastern Mediterranean region in the field of energy, particularly since the discovery of natural gas reserves offshore Israel, Cyprus and Egypt, indicates a growing need to develop the infrastructure to service the burgeoning oil and gas sector. In this respect, Cyprus has the greatest potential to be established as the energy hub of the Eastern Mediterranean region.

RPT Group can proudly point to a great portfolio of projects, having provided its services to most of the region’s oil majors over the last years. Through the Group’s collective experience and expertise in the sector, RPT is well-positioned to offer a wide range of services that cover the entire spectrum of demands of the companies active in the oil and gas industry. Our target is to provide top-quality integrated and constantly innovating business solutions, enabling our clients and partners to achieve their business objectives in safe, timely and cost-efficient manner.
