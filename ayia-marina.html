Ayia Marina Suites

A trip to Mount Athos would not be complete without the perfect location to rest after exploring its beauty and splendour.

The luxurious Ayia Marina Suites hotel is located in the heart
of Ouranoupoli, and overlooks the lush green Mount Athos and the Aegean coast, combining style, comfort and unparalleled hospitality. The hotel is also in a position to arrange private cruises and tours of Mount Athos.

Ayia Marina Suites, is certified according to the Standards ISO14001:2015, ISO9001:2015 by Hellenic Lloyd’s Register SA. .
