2019

Business Leader of the Year in Shipping

KEBE Business Leader Awards 2019

Awarded by the Cyprus Chamber of Commerce & Industry as the Business Leader in the Shipping Sector and recognized for the significant contribution both to the Cyprus Economy and Shipping Sector and for the Group’s fruitful investment activities in pioneer infrastructure projects inducing significant foreign investment in Cyprus in new fields of activities. As a result, Cyprus’s role in the region being upgraded to a hub for various services in the Shipping, Energy and Oil & Gas Sector.

Successful Investment Activity in Greece

Greek Cypriot Business Association Awards 2019

Awarded by the Greek-Cypriot Association for the Group’s activities and contribution to the Greek economy in the sector of Coastal Passenger and Cargo Transport by ships.
